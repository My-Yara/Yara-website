Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Yara is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your personal and financial information belongs to you, and we've built our technology to keep it that way.

Section 01

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, and authentication credentials
  • Documents You Share: Bills, statements, and service agreements you share with Yara, stored encrypted on your device and not on our servers
  • Preferences: Settings and customizations you configure

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (de-identified)
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for compatibility
  • Error Reports: Crash logs to improve stability
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Our website uses essential cookies and similar technologies; the Yara app does not use advertising cookies or third-party ad trackers

Information from Connected Financial Accounts

When you choose to connect a financial account through Plaid, we may receive:

  • Account Information: Account name, type, and balances
  • Transaction Data: Transaction history, descriptions, amounts, dates, and categories
  • Institution Details: Name of your financial institution
We do not store your bank statements or raw financial-account data. This information is pulled live from Plaid each time you open the app, used only during a consent session, and purged when the session ends. Only the insights Yara derives from it, such as a savings recommendation, are kept on your device in encrypted form; the underlying financial data is not retained after your session ends. We access only the categories of data you explicitly consent to.

Information You Share with Yara

When you ask Yara to help, you share information with Yara, such as your messages and documents like bills, statements, and service agreements. This information lives encrypted on your device. During a consent session, Yara's AI accesses only the specific information needed to act on your behalf; when the consent session ends, the access ends and the working session data is deleted from our servers, other than limited records such as audit and consent records or data we must keep by law, and your information remains only in encrypted form on your device. Yara's models cannot use your data once consent ends.

You control what you share. Please share only what Yara needs to act on your behalf, and avoid sharing highly sensitive information you don't need Yara to use: for example, government identification numbers, health or medical records, or account passwords. Do not share information about other people unless you have the authority to do so. You are responsible for the content you choose to share, and we may remove content that violates our Terms of Service.

Permissions the App Uses

With your permission, the app uses certain device features only for the feature you are using: the camera and photo library to capture and read your bills and documents, the microphone to place provider calls on your behalf, and notifications to alert you about renewals, savings, and negotiation outcomes. You can grant or revoke each permission at any time in your device settings. Revoking a permission turns off the related feature but does not otherwise affect the app.

Section 02

How We Protect Your Data

Your Data Lives on Your Device

Yara is built device-first. Your information is stored primarily on your own device, encrypted using AES-256. You may optionally enable an encrypted cloud backup (for example, to Yara Cloud, iCloud, Google Drive, or OneDrive) so your data is available across your devices. That backup is encrypted as well.

Encryption in Transit and at Rest

Data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. Access to decrypted data is limited to the active, consent-driven sessions you initiate.

Biometric Login

If you turn on Face ID or Touch ID to unlock the app, that authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. Yara never receives, collects, or stores your fingerprint, face scan, or any biometric identifier. You can turn off biometric unlock at any time in Settings.

Consent Controls What Yara Can See

Yara's AI works only on the data you allow, and only while you have granted consent. While you are using the app, your consent lets Yara's AI models access your data to fulfill your request. You can also grant consent for Yara to keep working on a task when you are not actively in the app. Even then, Yara uses your data only under that consent. When the consent ends, both the access and the working session data are removed from our servers, other than limited records such as audit and consent records or data we must keep by law, and your information remains only in encrypted form on your device.

During processing, the third-party AI models that power Yara receive only the data needed, for only as long as it takes to answer your request, and do not store your data. Consent sessions are limited in time (up to 24 hours), and you can end one at any time.

Financial Data Security

Data from connected financial accounts through Plaid is:

  • Transmitted over encrypted channels (TLS 1.3)
  • Not stored by Yara: it is pulled live from Plaid each time you open the app, used only during your consent session, and purged when the session ends
  • Accessed only transiently, in memory, to process your request
  • Insights Yara derives from it, such as a savings recommendation, are kept on your device in encrypted form; the financial data itself is not retained after your session ends
AES-256 Encryption TLS 1.3 in Transit
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered bill analysis and recommendations
  • Assist with provider negotiations and calls
  • Deliver spending insights and financial optimization suggestions based on connected account data
  • Power Yara's own AI to answer your questions and act on your behalf. This processing is handled by third-party AI models that do not store your data (see Section 05)
  • Improve the Service through de-identified, aggregated analytics
  • Communicate important updates about your account
🔒

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

We may also create and use aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably identify you, such as anonymized usage statistics, to operate and improve Yara. We do not attempt to re-identify it.

Section 04

Financial Account Connections (Plaid)

How Plaid Works

Yara uses Plaid Inc. to securely connect to your financial institutions. When you link an account, Plaid retrieves your financial data directly from your bank or financial institution through authorized APIs, never through screen scraping or credential storage.

Your Consent

Before connecting any financial account, you will be asked to provide clear, informed consent specifying which financial institution you are connecting, what categories of data Yara will access, and how your data will be used. You control what Yara can see during each session, and you can change or withdraw your consent, or connect or disconnect accounts, at any time through the app. We maintain records of the consents you provide.

How We Use Connected Account Data

Data from connected financial accounts is used solely to:

  • Surface transaction history and spending patterns within the app
  • Deliver AI-powered spending insights and savings recommendations
  • Support bill analysis and provider negotiation features
  • Provide personalized financial optimization
We do not use your connected account data for advertising, and we do not share it with unrelated third parties. Plaid's collection, use, and handling of your data is governed by Plaid's End User Privacy Policy.

Not a Consumer Report

Information obtained through Plaid is not a consumer report under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), and Yara does not use it to determine your eligibility for credit, insurance, employment, housing, or any other FCRA-covered purpose.

Section 05

Third-Party AI Processing

How Yara's AI Processes Your Request

Yara shares only the data needed for the specific feature you are using, and only for as long as it takes to process your request. Yara is an AI product that answers your questions, analyzes your bills, and advocates on your behalf. To handle some requests, Yara's AI draws on trusted third-party AI models that process your data in a secure, enterprise environment solely to generate a response. Depending on what you ask, this can include:

  • Your chat messages: the text of your conversations with Yara
  • Bills and documents: the bills, statements, and service agreements you share with Yara
  • Financial and transaction details: account and transaction information relevant to the task you've asked Yara to perform
  • Provider and negotiation context: details about the provider relationship needed to advocate or negotiate on your behalf
  • Search queries: questions Yara sends to find answers or recommendations
  • Provider-portal content: screenshots and page content from provider websites when you ask Yara to act on a provider portal for you

These third-party AI models do not store your data. Your information is processed to answer your request and is then discarded.

🔒

Under our agreements with these models, your data is not used to train them and is not retained after your request is processed, apart from any short-term operational handling needed to deliver the response or any retention required by law. Yara uses an enterprise processing tier governed by contractual no-training and no-retention terms.

When Yara Makes Calls on Your Behalf

When you ask Yara to contact a provider for you, Yara's AI may place a voice call and create a recording or transcript of that call so Yara can complete your request and keep a record of what was agreed. At the start of the call, Yara identifies itself as an automated assistant acting on your behalf and states that the call may be recorded and transcribed, so that all parties are on notice before the conversation continues. Call transcripts receive the same protection as the other documents in your account: they are stored encrypted on your device, accessed only during a consent session, and are not used to train any AI models.

AI Outputs and Your Control

Yara is an AI product. While we work to make Yara accurate and helpful, AI outputs such as savings estimates, bill summaries, and negotiation suggestions may be incomplete or incorrect, and they are not professional financial, legal, or tax advice. Yara does not take actions that have a legal or similarly significant effect on you through automated processing alone: actions that change your provider relationships, such as cancelling, switching, or agreeing to new terms, require your review and consent before Yara acts. We do not use human reviewers to read your conversations as a routine matter; limited human review may occur only to investigate a security issue or suspected abuse, or where required by law. You may ask us how an AI-assisted recommendation about your providers was generated, and you may request human review of any such recommendation before acting on it.

Equal Protection by Third Parties

Our service providers (including the third-party AI models that process your requests and our infrastructure and hosting providers) are contractually bound to protect your data to a standard consistent with this Privacy Policy, to use it only to provide services to Yara, and not to sell it or use it for their own purposes. Plaid Inc. is an independent company that also processes your data under its own End User Privacy Policy; Plaid's use of your information is governed by that policy in addition to its agreement with us.

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate the Service, including:

  • Plaid Inc.: Financial account connectivity and transaction data retrieval
  • Third-party AI models: Process your requests to power Yara's AI features; they do not store your data
  • Infrastructure and hosting providers: Secure storage and delivery of the Service

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for the purposes of providing services to Yara.

Yara may link to or interact with third-party websites and services, including provider websites. We do not control those services and are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

Revoking Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time. You may end an active AI session and purge its cached data directly in the app, disconnect any connected financial account in the app at any time, and turn off AI features in your settings. Withdrawing consent stops further processing of the affected data, including any sharing with third-party AI models, going forward.

Requesting Deletion

You can request deletion of your data in two ways:

  • In the app: Use the account and data controls in Settings to delete connected-account data or your entire account.
  • By email: Contact us at privacy@my-yara.com and we will process your request.

When you disconnect a financial account, the associated data is removed from our systems. When you delete your account, all of your data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain limited records to comply with law. Any encrypted backups you have stored in your own cloud account (such as iCloud, Google Drive, or OneDrive) are under your control and can be deleted by you at any time. Because the third-party AI models that power Yara process your data transiently and do not retain it after a request is processed, they do not keep a separate stored copy of it.

Communication Preferences

You can opt out of promotional emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any such email or by contacting us. We will still send you essential service and account messages.
