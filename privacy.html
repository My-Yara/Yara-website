Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026 Last Updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Yara is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your personal and financial information belongs to you, and we've built our technology to keep it that way.

Section 01

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, and authentication credentials
  • Financial Documents: Bills, statements, and service agreements you upload
  • Preferences: Settings and customizations you configure

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for compatibility
  • Error Reports: Crash logs to improve stability

Information from Connected Financial Accounts

When you choose to connect a financial account through Plaid, we may receive:

  • Account Information: Account name, type, and balances
  • Transaction Data: Transaction history, descriptions, amounts, dates, and categories
  • Institution Details: Name of your financial institution
This data is retrieved through Plaid Inc.'s secure API services. We only access the categories of data you explicitly consent to when connecting your account. Plaid's collection and use of your data is also subject to Plaid's End User Privacy Policy.
Section 02

How We Protect Your Data

End-to-End Encryption

All your personal information is encrypted on your device before transmission. Our servers store only encrypted data that we cannot read without your active participation.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Your sensitive data is encrypted with keys derived from your credentials. Yara's servers cannot decrypt your stored data without your cooperation.

Consent-Driven AI Sessions

When you enable AI features, you grant temporary access for a limited time (1–24 hours). After the session expires, decrypted data is automatically purged from our servers.

Financial Data Security

Data retrieved from connected financial accounts through Plaid is:

  • Transmitted over encrypted channels (TLS 1.3)
  • Stored only in encrypted form in our persistent databases
  • Accessible to our AI only during active, consent-driven sessions
  • Never stored in plaintext on disk
AES-256 Encryption TLS 1.3 in Transit AICPA Trust Criteria NIST 800-53 ISO 27002
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered bill analysis and recommendations
  • Assist with provider negotiations and calls
  • Deliver spending insights and financial optimization suggestions based on connected account data
  • Improve the Service through anonymized analytics
  • Communicate important updates about your account
🔒

We never sell your personal information to third parties — period.

Section 04

Financial Account Connections (Plaid)

How Plaid Works

Yara uses Plaid Inc. to securely connect to your financial institutions. When you link an account, Plaid retrieves your financial data directly from your bank or financial institution through authorized APIs — never through screen scraping or credential storage.

Your Consent

Before connecting any financial account, you will be asked to provide clear, informed consent specifying which financial institution you are connecting, what categories of data Yara will access, and how your data will be used. You may connect or disconnect accounts at any time through the app.

How We Use Connected Account Data

Data from connected financial accounts is used solely to:

  • Surface transaction history and spending patterns within the app
  • Deliver AI-powered spending insights and savings recommendations
  • Support bill analysis and provider negotiation features
  • Provide personalized financial optimization
We do not use your connected account data for advertising, and we do not share it with unrelated third parties. Plaid's collection, use, and handling of your data is governed by Plaid's End User Privacy Policy.
Section 05

AI and Third-Party Processors

AI Model Usage

When you use AI features, some data may be processed by AI models to provide responses. This data is:

  • Never used to train AI models (unless you explicitly opt in)
  • Not retained by AI providers after processing
  • Subject to our privacy safeguards

Service Providers

We work with trusted service providers who help us operate the Service, including:

  • Plaid Inc. — Financial account connectivity and transaction data retrieval
  • AI Model Providers — Processing of AI-powered features

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for the purposes of providing services to Yara.
